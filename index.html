Delicious recipes for every occasion

Chocolate Cake

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup oil

Instructions:

Mix dry ingredients, add wet, bake at 350°F for 30 mins.

Strawberry Cheesecake

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup graham cracker crumbs
  • 8 oz cream cheese
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 1 cup strawberries
  • 1 tsp vanilla

Instructions:

Crush crackers for crust, mix filling, top with strawberries, chill for 2 hours.

Vanilla Cupcakes

Ingredients:

  • 1 ½ cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • ½ cup butter
  • ½ cup milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla

Instructions:

Mix butter and sugar, add eggs and vanilla. Add dry ingredients and milk. Bake at 350°F (180°C) for 18-20 mins.

Brownies

Instructions:

Crush crackers for crust, mix filling, top with strawberries, chill for 2 hours.

Chocolate Chip Cookies

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup flour
  • ½ cup butter
  • ½ cup sugar
  • ½ cup chocolate chips
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp vanilla

Instructions:

Mix butter and sugar, add egg and vanilla. Add flour and baking soda, then chocolate chips. Bake at 350°F (180°C) for 10–12 mins.

Caramel Custard

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups milk
  • 3 eggs
  • ½ cup sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • ¼ cup caramel sugar syrup

Instructions:

Pour caramel syrup into bowl. Mix milk, eggs, sugar, and vanilla and pour over caramel. Steam or bake for 25–30 mins until set.

Red Velvet Cupcakes

Ingredients:

  • 1 ½ cups flour
  • ½ cup sugar
  • ½ cup butter
  • 1 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 2 egg
  • 1 tsp red food color
  • ½ cup milk

Instructions:

Mix wet ingredients, then add dry. Stir until smooth. Bake at 350°F (180°C) for 20 mins and frost if desired.

Apple Pie

Ingredients:

  • 2 apples sliced
  • 1 cup flour
  • ½ cup butter
  • ¼ cup sugar
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions:

Fill dough with apples, sugar, and cinnamon mixture. Seal edges and bake at 375°F (190°C) for 30–35 mins until golden.

Rasmalai

Ingredients:

  • 6 rasgullas
  • 500 ml milk
  • 3 tbsp sugar
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom powder
  • Chopped nuts (almonds, pistachios)
  • Saffron strands (optional)

Instructions

Boil milk with sugar, saffron and cardamom until slightly thick. Squeeze rasgullas gently and add to milk. Simmer for 5 minutes and chill before serving.

Pav Bhaji

Ingredients:

  • 2 potatoes (boiled & mashed)
  • 1/2 cup cauliflower (boiled & mashed)
  • 1/4 cup green peas
  • 1 onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (finely chopped)
  • 1 capsicum (finely chopped)
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tsp pav bhaji masala
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Salt to taste
  • Pav buns (4)

Instructions

Heat butter, sauté onion and ginger-garlic paste. Add tomatoes, capsicum and spices and cook until soft. Add boiled veggies and mash well while cooking. Simmer for 5–7 minutes. Toast pav with butter and serve hot with bhaji, onion and lemon.

Ingredients:

Instructions

Lemon Mojito

Ingredients:

  • 1 lemon (cut into slices)
  • 6-7 mint leaves
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • Ice cubes
  • Chilled soda or water

Instructions

Muddle lemon, mint and sugar. Add ice cubes and pour soda or water. Stir well and serve fresh.
